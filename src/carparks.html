To help people decide when to visit the city centre this beta test site uses open source data to gauge pedestrian density under social distancing conditions on Northumberland St and, where available, also provides an indication of remaining off-street parking capacity.

Car Park symbols in Green/Amber/Red indicate remaining capacity.

Car Park symbols in Grey show total capacity only.
